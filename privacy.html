Auto Refresh Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Auto Refresh ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our browser extension ("Extension"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your data is handled.

Information We Collect

We collect and process the following information to provide and improve our Extension:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous data about how you use the Extension, including features used and settings preferences.
  • Technical Information: Browser type and version, operating system, screen resolution, and language preferences.
  • Error Reports: Information about any errors or crashes you encounter while using the Extension.

Note: You can opt out of analytics tracking completely through the Extension settings page.

Advertising and Tracking

The free version of Auto Refresh includes advertisements. Please be aware of the following:

  • Third-party advertising networks may use cookies and similar technologies to collect information for ad targeting purposes.
  • You can choose the "Minimal Ads" option in settings to reduce the frequency of advertisements and minimize tracking data collected.
  • Premium subscribers do not receive advertisements.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information Type How We Use It
Extension settings To save your preferences and provide the extension functionality
Usage statistics To improve the extension features and performance
Payment information To process transactions and maintain subscription records
Error reports To fix bugs and improve stability
Ad interaction data To optimize advertisement relevance and measure campaign effectiveness

3. Data Storage and Sharing

3.1 Local Storage

Most of your settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data syncs across your devices if you have enabled browser sync.

3.2 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Paddle: Processes payments for premium subscriptions as the Merchant of Record
  • Google Analytics: Collects anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the extension
  • Affiliate Partners: The free version of the extension displays advertisements from our affiliate partners. Clicking on these ads will send you to partner websites through tracking links that identify our extension as the referrer.

3.3 Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate the extension (e.g., payment processors)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Anonymous, aggregated usage data for analytics purposes
  • Ad impression and click data with our advertising partners (in anonymous, aggregated form)

Your Choices

You have several options to control your privacy:

  • Analytics Opt-Out: Disable usage data collection in the Extension settings.
  • Minimal Ads: Choose to see fewer advertisements with reduced tracking in the free version.
  • Premium Subscription: Upgrade to remove all advertisements.
  • Data Request: Contact us to request a copy of your personal data or to request deletion of your data.

5. Premium Features and Ad-Free Experience

The free version of Auto Refresh includes advertisements. When you upgrade to a premium plan:

  • All advertisements are removed
  • No ad tracking or analytics related to advertisements will be collected
  • We still collect basic usage statistics to maintain and improve the extension, unless you opt out
  • Your payment information is handled securely by our payment processor (Paddle)

6. Children's Privacy

Our Extension is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes through the Extension or via email. Your continued use of the Extension after such modifications constitutes your acknowledgment of the modified Privacy Policy.
