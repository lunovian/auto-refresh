Auto Refresh Refund Policy

This Refund Policy outlines the procedures and conditions for refunds related to Auto Refresh premium plans and donations. We strive to ensure complete customer satisfaction, but we also need clear guidelines to handle refund requests.

Summary: We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all premium plans. Refund requests must be submitted within this period to be eligible for consideration.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Auto Refresh offers refunds under the following conditions:

  • Time Window: Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of the initial purchase.
  • Valid Reasons: Acceptable reasons for refunds include:
    • The extension does not function as described
    • Technical issues that prevent the use of premium features
    • Accidental or duplicate purchases
  • Complete Information: Refund requests must include the original transaction ID and the email address used for purchase.

2. Refund Process

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team via email at support@autorefresh.example.com with the subject line "Refund Request"
  2. Include the following information in your email:
    • Transaction ID (found in your purchase confirmation email)
    • Date of purchase
    • The email address used for the purchase
    • Reason for requesting a refund
  3. Our team will review your request and respond within 3 business days
  4. If approved, refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-10 business days

3. Refund Scenarios by Plan Type

Plan Type Refund Policy
Monthly Subscription Full refund if requested within 14 days of the initial purchase or the latest renewal. No prorated refunds after this period.
Yearly Subscription Full refund if requested within 14 days of purchase. Prorated refunds may be considered on a case-by-case basis after this period.
Lifetime Plan Full refund if requested within 14 days of purchase. No refunds after this period except in exceptional circumstances.
Donations As donations are voluntary contributions, they are generally non-refundable. However, accidental donations may be refunded at our discretion.

4. Non-Refundable Scenarios

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Requests made after the 14-day refund window has expired
  • If you've violated our Terms of Service
  • If the reason for the refund request is not related to the functionality of Auto Refresh (e.g., "I changed my mind" or "I no longer need it")
  • If you've already received a refund for a previous purchase of Auto Refresh premium features

5. Subscription Cancellations

Canceling a subscription is different from requesting a refund:

  • You can cancel your subscription at any time through the settings page of the Auto Refresh extension
  • Cancellation will stop future recurring payments
  • You will continue to have access to premium features until the end of your current billing period
  • Canceling your subscription does not automatically issue a refund for payments already made

6. Payment Processor Policies

Auto Refresh uses Paddle as our payment processor. In some cases, Paddle's refund policies may apply in addition to our own. Paddle may charge administrative fees for processing refunds, which may be deducted from the refund amount.

7. Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective when posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy whenever you make a purchase.
